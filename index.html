Markovian Trump Tweets

Welcome to the Markovian Trump Tweet Generator! Using a straightforward artifical intelligence system called a Markov Chain and a wonderful database of the president's gold nuggets from the Trump Twitter Archive, we've strung together the juiciest bits of information to possibly hypothesize what comes next for our country.

But don't get too excited; chances are, you'll just get the same nonsense you can expect from @realDonaldTrump!
